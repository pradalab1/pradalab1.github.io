Welcome to the Provable Responsible AI and Data Analytics Lab

‹ ›

The Provable Responsible AI and Data Analytics Lab (PRADA Lab) is a research group within the Division of Computer, Electrical, and Mathematical Sciences and Engineering (CEMSE) at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST). Established in 2021, the lab is currently led by Professor Di Wang.

PRADA Lab focuses on trustworthy machine learning and large models, with research areas including privacy, interpretability, adversarial robustness, and fairness. The lab is also engaged in AI for Science, with applications in optics, optical neural networks, healthcare, and material science.

Presently, the lab has more than 10 members, including 1 visiting professor, 4 postdoctoral researchers, 9 PhD students, 3 collaborative PhD students, and 2 Master's students.
